“I create art that explores the idea of
femininity by combining figures of
women with elements of nature. My
main goal is to express a wide range
of diversity within these different
figures and showcase the wide
spectrum of being “feminine”. Natural
elements are used to help express this
diversity by demonstrating how there
is so much variety that occurs without
a second thought. I believe that nature
is unapologetic in its beauty. It simply
looks how it does without explanation.
I want my art to reflect that sentiment
as well."
